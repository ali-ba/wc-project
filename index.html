TEAMS

The 2018 FIFA World Cup qualification process was a series of tournaments organised by the six FIFA confederations to decide 31 of the 32 teams which will play in the 2018 FIFA World Cup, with Russia qualifying automatically as hosts. For the first time in World Cup history, all 210 eligible national teams registered for the preliminary competition.
